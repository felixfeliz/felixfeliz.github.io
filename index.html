Dr. Felix Scholz

Applied Geometry

Johannes Kepler University Linz

I am a researcher at the Institute of Applied Geometry of Johannes Kepler University Linz. My research interests are at the crossroads of geometric modeling, numerical analysis and geometric deep learning.
